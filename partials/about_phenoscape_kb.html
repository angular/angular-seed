Ontologies

Logical relationships expressed in ontologies tie together everything in the Knowledgebase and drive functionality. The KB includes and builds on these community-developed ontologies:

  • Uberon ontology of metazoan anatomy
  • Phenotype and trait ontology (PATO)
  • Biospatial ontology (BSPO)
  • Zebrafish anatomy (ZFA)
  • Xenopus anatomy (XAO)
  • Mouse developmental anatomy (EMAPA)
  • Mammalian phenotype ontology (MP)
  • Human phenotype ontology (HP)
  • OBO relations ontology (RO)
  • Gene ontology (GO)
